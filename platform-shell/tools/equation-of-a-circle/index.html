TEACHER INSTRUCTIONS
Teacher Instructions

Topics Covered

  • Finding the centre and radius from a circle equation.
  • Writing the equation of a circle from a centre and radius.
  • Converting expanded circle equations into standard form.
  • Finding intersections of a circle with a line or another circle.
  • Finding tangent equations and tangent lengths.

Core Formula

The standard form is (x - a)2 + (y - b)2 = r2, where (a, b) is the centre and r is the radius.

Teaching Notes

  • Encourage students to identify the centre by reading the signs carefully.
  • For expanded equations, complete the square separately for x and y.
  • For two circles, subtract the equations first to create a linear equation.
  • For tangents, connect radius and tangent as perpendicular lines.
