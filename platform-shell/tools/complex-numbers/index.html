Complex Numbers

Overview

This Further Maths tool builds fluency with complex numbers in Cartesian form, polar form, De Moivre's theorem, roots, equations, and loci on the Argand diagram.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: arithmetic, conjugates, division, modulus, and basic algebra with i.
  • Level 2: modulus, argument, Cartesian-polar conversion, and polar multiplication or division.
  • Level 3: De Moivre's theorem, powers, roots, and quadratics with complex roots.
  • Level 4: Argand loci, perpendicular bisectors, half-planes, argument rays, and mixed exam-style questions.

Teaching Focus

  • Keep real and imaginary parts separate when simplifying.
  • Use i^2 = -1 every time it appears in expansion.
  • For division, multiply numerator and denominator by the conjugate of the denominator.
  • For polar form, multiplication adds arguments and division subtracts arguments.
  • For loci, translate the complex statement into coordinates on the Argand diagram.
