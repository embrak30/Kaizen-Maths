Upper and Lower Bounds

Overview

This tool practises error intervals and calculations with upper and lower bounds. It is designed for GCSE, IGCSE, and CSEC-style number work.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: find lower bounds, upper bounds, and error intervals from rounded values.
  • Level 2: combine bounds in sums, differences, products, and quotients.
  • Level 3: apply bounds to perimeter, area, speed, density, and other formulae.
  • Level 4: percentage error, reverse bounds, and mixed exam-style questions.

Teaching Focus

  • Always start by writing the error interval for each rounded quantity.
  • For a maximum, choose the upper bound of quantities that increase the result.
  • For a minimum, choose the lower bound of quantities that increase the result.
  • For subtraction and division, think carefully about which value makes the result larger or smaller.
