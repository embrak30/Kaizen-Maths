Teacher Instructions

What this tool is for

Interactive practice with derivatives of inverse trigonometric functions and their applications at points, including tangents and normals.

Level Overview

  • Level 1: Core derivatives of inverse trig functions using chain and power rules, plus evaluating y' at a given x.
  • Level 2: Tangent and normal lines at specified points for functions built from inverse trig expressions.

Common Mistakes

  • Omitting the inner derivative in the chain rule.
  • Dropping the negative sign for cos^-1, cot^-1, and csc^-1.
  • Forgetting absolute values in sec^-1 and csc^-1 derivatives.
