📚 Teacher Instructions

Overview

This tool focuses on PEMDAS only. There are three levels, all rendered with KaTeX for clear mathematical notation.

Levels

  • Level 1 — Basic Operations: Only positive numbers. Every problem has at least two operations. No brackets yet. Designed to stress test precedence.
  • Level 2 — Brackets + Three Operations: Positive numbers with brackets. Each expression includes three operations and a Mixed type that may include four.
  • Level 3 — Introduce Negatives: Three to four operations, brackets allowed, and negative numbers introduced.

How Steps Are Shown

  • Short sentences with one idea per step.
  • Steps follow PEMDAS: parentheses, exponents, multiplication and division left to right, addition and subtraction left to right.
  • Intermediate results appear on centered math lines.

Standards & Alignment

  • UK National Curriculum (KS2–KS3):
    • Use order of operations including parentheses and exponents.
    • Evaluate numerical expressions; apply place value and arithmetic strategies.
  • Common Core (CCSS):
    • 5.OA.A.1–2: Use parentheses and evaluate numerical expressions.
    • 6.EE.A.1–2: Write and evaluate numerical expressions with whole-number exponents; apply order of operations.
    • 7.NS.A: Apply properties of operations to rational numbers.

Timer Suggestions

  • 2 minutes for quick practice.
  • 5 minutes for full worked solutions.
  • Encourage learners to state the rule used at each step.
