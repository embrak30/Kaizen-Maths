Teacher Instructions

Purpose

This tool generates practice on moments, turning effects, balance, and reaction forces in mechanics.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: Calculate a moment and decide whether it acts clockwise or anticlockwise.
  • Level 2: Use the principle of moments to find unknown forces, distances, and masses.
  • Level 3: Use moments and vertical equilibrium to find reaction forces on beams.

Teaching Notes

  • Moment = force × perpendicular distance from the pivot.
  • For equilibrium, total clockwise moment equals total anticlockwise moment.
  • For supported beams, combine moments with vertical force balance.
