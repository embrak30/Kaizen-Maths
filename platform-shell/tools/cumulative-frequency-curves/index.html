TEACHER INSTRUCTIONS
Teacher Instructions

Purpose

This tool generates practice on cumulative frequency tables, cumulative frequency curves, medians, quartiles, percentiles, and estimates from curves.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: Complete cumulative frequency tables and identify the plotting coordinates.
  • Level 2: Interpret cumulative frequency curves one skill at a time.
  • Level 3: Use curves for exam-style table completion, summaries, and reverse percentile questions.

Teaching Notes

  • Cumulative frequency means a running total.
  • Plot cumulative frequency against the upper class boundary.
  • The curve starts at the lower boundary of the first class with cumulative frequency 0.
  • For medians and quartiles, use positions based on the total frequency.
  • Reading from a curve gives an estimate.
