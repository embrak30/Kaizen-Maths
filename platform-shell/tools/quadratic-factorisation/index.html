Teacher Instructions

Getting Started

This tool demonstrates systematic quadratic factorisation. Students practise extracting common factors, recognising special forms, and factorising trinomials.

Level Guide

  • Level 1 - Basic: Common factors, difference of squares, perfect square trinomials.
  • Level 2 - Advanced: Standard trinomials, grouping, and mixed expressions.

Teaching Strategies

  • Always check for common factors first.
  • After factoring, check whether the expression can be factored further.
  • Use expansion to verify the final answer.

Common Mistakes

  • Forgetting common factors.
  • Stopping before complete factorisation.
  • Mixing up sign patterns in special forms.
  • Not checking by expanding.
