Teacher Instructions

Purpose

This tool generates histogram practice focused on frequency density, completing grouped-frequency tables, reading histograms, and interpreting areas.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: Complete frequency-density tables and connect class width, frequency, and frequency density.
  • Level 2: Interpret histograms, including modal class, total frequency, partial intervals, and percentages.
  • Level 3: Complete tables from histograms, estimate median and quartiles, and answer mixed exam-style questions.

Teaching Notes

  • In a histogram, the area of each bar represents the frequency.
  • The vertical scale is frequency density, not frequency.
  • Frequency density = frequency ÷ class width.
  • Frequency = class width × frequency density.
  • For partial intervals, assume the data are evenly spread within the class interval.
