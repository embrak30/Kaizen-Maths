TEACHER INSTRUCTIONS
Teacher Instructions

Purpose

This tool builds fluency with decimals through structured practice. Students reason about place value, compare and round decimals, convert between fractions and decimals, and solve operations and multi-step problems.

Level Guide

  • Level 1 - Foundations: Place value, comparing, ordering, rounding, fraction to decimal, expanded form.
  • Level 2 - Operations: Addition, subtraction, powers of 10, multiplication, and division with decimals.
  • Level 3 - Applications: Multi-step expressions, error spotting, and real-world contexts.

Teaching Moves

  • Insist on place value language: tenth, hundredth, thousandth.
  • Model vertical alignment for operations.
  • Use estimates to check reasonableness.
  • Promote stepwise solutions before calculating.

Common Misconceptions

  • Thinking 3.5 is less than 3.45 because 45 is larger than 5.
  • Applying rounding rules to the wrong digit.
  • Misaligning decimal points in addition and subtraction.
  • Moving the decimal point the wrong way for powers of 10.
