Advanced Matrices

Overview

This Further Maths tool extends matrix work into transformations, eigenvalues, diagonalisation, Cayley-Hamilton applications, powers, and systems of equations.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: Matrix transformations, composite transformations, and fixed lines.
  • Level 2: Characteristic equations, eigenvalues, eigenvectors, and diagonalisation.
  • Level 3: Cayley-Hamilton, inverse matrices from the characteristic equation, and powers of matrices.
  • Level 4: Matrix equations and systems, including 3 by 3 triangular systems.

Teaching Focus

  • For transformations, order matters: applying A then B gives the combined matrix BA.
  • For eigenvectors, solve (A - λI)v = 0.
  • For Cayley-Hamilton, replace λ by A in the characteristic equation.
  • For systems, each column can be solved as a matrix equation.
