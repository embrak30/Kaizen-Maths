Teacher Instructions

What this tool is for

Interactive practice for differentiating trigonometric functions using power, chain, product, and quotient rules. It scaffolds from core fluency to multi-step compositions and provides stepwise model solutions.

Level Overview

  • Level 1: Core sin, cos, tan, sec, csc, cot derivatives with linear inners and trig powers.
  • Level 2: Composite inners, products of trig, and quotients of trig only.
  • Level 3: Nested chains, powers of trig with extra factors, and three-factor products.
  • Level 4: Exam-style blends requiring rule selection and algebraic tidying.

Common Mistakes

  • Missing the inner derivative in the chain rule.
  • Incorrect signs for cos, csc, and cot derivatives.
  • Losing squared factors such as sec^2 u and csc^2 u.
  • Choosing product when quotient is required, or vice versa.
