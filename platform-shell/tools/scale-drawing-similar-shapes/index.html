Scale & Similarity Guidance

Overview

This tool supports scale drawing, map scale, similar shapes, similar triangles, and area or volume ratio problems.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: scale drawings, map scales, actual length, and drawing length.
  • Level 2: similar shapes, scale factors, and perimeter ratios.
  • Level 3: similar triangles and missing sides using corresponding lengths.
  • Level 4: area and volume ratios for similar shapes and solids.

Teaching Focus

  • Keep units consistent before using a scale.
  • Identify corresponding sides before forming a ratio.
  • Length scale factor affects lengths and perimeters.
  • Area scale factor is the square of the length scale factor.
  • Volume scale factor is the cube of the length scale factor.
