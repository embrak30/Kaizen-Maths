TEACHER INSTRUCTIONS
Teacher Instructions

Topics Covered

  • Simplifying algebraic fractions by factorising and cancelling common factors.
  • Multiplying and dividing algebraic fractions.
  • Adding and subtracting algebraic fractions using common denominators.
  • Solving equations involving algebraic fractions.

Teaching Notes

  • Students should factorise first, then cancel whole factors only.
  • Emphasise that terms cannot be cancelled unless they are part of a common factor.
  • For equations, multiply by the lowest common denominator and check excluded values.
