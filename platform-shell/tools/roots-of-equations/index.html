Roots of Equations

Overview

This tool builds practice with roots of polynomial equations, including the relationships between roots and coefficients, transformed roots, and forming equations from given roots.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: Quadratic roots, sums, products, and simple symmetric expressions.
  • Level 2: Cubic roots and Vieta relationships.
  • Level 3: Equations whose roots are shifted, scaled, reciprocal, or squared versions of the original roots.
  • Level 4: Repeated roots, parameter questions, complex conjugate roots, and exam-style mixed problems.

Teaching Focus

  • For ax² + bx + c = 0, use α + β = −b/a and αβ = c/a.
  • For ax³ + bx² + cx + d = 0, use α + β + γ = −b/a, αβ + αγ + βγ = c/a, and αβγ = −d/a.
  • When roots are transformed, find the new sum and product before writing the new equation.
