Prisms Guidance

Overview

This tool covers volume and surface area of cuboids and triangular prisms. It builds from direct formula use to reverse and mixed questions.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: Volume of cuboids and triangular prisms.
  • Level 2: Surface area of cuboids and triangular prisms.
  • Level 3: Reverse problems and mixed prism calculations.

Key Formulae

  • Volume of prism: cross-section area × length.
  • Cuboid volume: length × width × height.
  • Cuboid surface area: 2(lw + lh + wh).
  • Triangular prism surface area: two triangles + three rectangles.

Common Misconceptions

  • Using area units for volume or volume units for surface area.
  • Forgetting the two triangular ends on triangular prisms.
  • Using the sloping side as the triangle height.
  • Confusing prism length with the height of the cross-section.
