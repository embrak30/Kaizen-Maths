TEACHER INSTRUCTIONS
Teacher Instructions

Topics Covered

  • Basic common factors: numerical, variable, and combined factors.
  • Highest common factor with powers and multiple variables.
  • Difference of two squares, including hidden common factors and bracketed expressions.
  • Quadratic and cubic complete factorisation.

Teaching Notes

  • Students should always check for a common factor before using a special pattern.
  • Difference of two squares should be recognised as A2 - B2 = (A - B)(A + B).
  • For advanced expressions, the final answer should be factorised completely.
