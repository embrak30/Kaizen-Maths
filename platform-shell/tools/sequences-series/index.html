Teacher Instructions

Overview

This tool focuses on Sequences and Series. It scaffolds from arithmetic sequences to geometric series with exact worked solutions.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: Arithmetic nth terms, common difference, sums, inverse problems, and sigma notation.
  • Level 2: Geometric nth terms, ratios, partial sums, sums to infinity, and geometric sigma notation.

Common Misconceptions

  • Confusing a sequence with a series.
  • Treating a geometric pattern as additive rather than multiplicative.
  • Using a sum to infinity when the ratio does not get smaller in size.
