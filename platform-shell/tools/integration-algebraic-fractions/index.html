Integration of Algebraic Fractions

Overview

This tool develops integration of rational expressions by rewriting algebraic fractions into forms that can be integrated directly.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: Linear denominators, logarithmic derivative forms, and simple splitting.
  • Level 2: Cancelling factors and polynomial division before integrating.
  • Level 3: Partial fractions with distinct, repeated, and improper rational expressions.
  • Level 4: Quadratic denominators, completing the square, logarithm and inverse tangent forms.

Teaching Focus

  • Students should rewrite the fraction before integrating.
  • Look for numerator as a multiple of the derivative of the denominator.
  • Improper algebraic fractions need division before integration.
  • Quadratic denominators may require completing the square and an inverse tangent result.
