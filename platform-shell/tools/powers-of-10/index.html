Teacher Instructions

Getting Started

This tool builds understanding of powers of 10 through multiplication, division, and standard form operations.

Level Structure

  • Level 1: Whole numbers and decimals multiplied by powers of 10.
  • Level 2: Whole numbers and decimals divided by powers of 10.
  • Level 3: Express, evaluate, and calculate with standard form numbers.

Key Ideas

  • Multiplying by powers of 10 moves the decimal point to the right.
  • Dividing by powers of 10 moves the decimal point to the left.
  • Standard form is A x 10^n where 1 <= A < 10.
  • Use index laws: 10^a x 10^b = 10^(a+b) and 10^a / 10^b = 10^(a-b).

Common Mistakes

  • Moving the decimal point in the wrong direction.
  • Miscounting the number of places.
  • Forgetting zero placeholders.
  • Leaving a standard form coefficient outside the range 1 to 10.
