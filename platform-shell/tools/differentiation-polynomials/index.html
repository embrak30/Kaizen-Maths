Teacher Instructions

Key Principles

  • Power rule: d/dx[xn] = nxn-1.
  • Constant multiple rule: d/dx[cf(x)] = c f'(x).
  • Sum and difference rule: Differentiate each term separately.
  • Constant rule: d/dx[c] = 0.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: Single terms, coefficients, constants, and linear terms.
  • Level 2: Quadratic, cubic, and higher-degree polynomials.
  • Level 3: Gradients at points, tangent equations, and normal equations.

Teaching Focus

  • Multiply by the original power, then reduce the power by one.
  • Differentiate term by term before simplifying.
  • Connect f'(a) to the gradient of the tangent at x = a.
