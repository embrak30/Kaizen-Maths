Volumes of Revolution

Overview

This tool generates integration practice for volumes formed by rotating regions around an axis. It focuses on using the correct volume formula, squaring the radius expression, integrating, and substituting bounds carefully.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: Disc method about the x-axis.
  • Level 2: Expanding before integrating.
  • Level 3: Washer method between two curves.
  • Level 4: Rotation about the y-axis and parameter problems.

Teaching Focus

  • Students must identify the radius before writing the integral.
  • The radius is squared, so brackets and expansion need care.
  • For washers, subtract the inner radius squared from the outer radius squared.
  • For the y-axis form used here, write the curve as x in terms of y and integrate with respect to y.
