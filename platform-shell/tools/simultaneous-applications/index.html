Teacher Instructions

Getting Started

This tool generates mixed application problems that require simultaneous equations. Worked solutions use elimination and exact integer or fractional answers.

Levels

  • Level 1 - Basic Applications: Tickets, mixtures, distance, work, coins, bundles, supply/demand, and line intersections.
  • Level 2 - Complex Applications: Three-variable contexts and problems that lead naturally to quadratics.

Assessment Focus

  • Translating word problems into equations.
  • Executing elimination accurately, including scaling and signs.
  • Working with fractions instead of decimals.
  • Interpreting solutions in context.

Standards and Alignment

  • UK KS3-KS4: Simultaneous equations, algebraic modelling, and basic quadratic relationships.
  • CCSS 8.EE.C.8: Analyze and solve pairs of simultaneous linear equations.
  • CCSS A.CED.A.1-3: Create equations that describe relationships.
