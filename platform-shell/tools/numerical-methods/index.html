Numerical Methods

Overview

This tool supports A-level and Further Maths numerical methods, including locating roots, iteration, Newton-Raphson, numerical integration, and Euler's method.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: Sign changes, bisection, and fixed-point iteration.
  • Level 2: Newton-Raphson formula, one-step updates, and repeated iterations.
  • Level 3: Trapezium rule from tables and from functions.
  • Level 4: Euler's method for first-order differential equations.

Teaching Focus

  • Students should show substituted values before giving decimal approximations.
  • Root-location methods depend on a change of sign over an interval.
  • Newton-Raphson needs both f(x) and f'(x) evaluated at the current approximation.
  • For trapezium rule, the first and last ordinates are counted once; interior ordinates are doubled.
