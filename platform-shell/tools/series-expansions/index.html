Series Expansions

Overview

This Further Maths tool develops Maclaurin series, Taylor series, substitution into standard expansions, products of series, limits, approximations, and simple series solutions.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: Standard Maclaurin series and building a series from derivatives at zero.
  • Level 2: Substitution, products, quotients, and approximations using Maclaurin series.
  • Level 3: Taylor series about a point and approximations near the expansion point.
  • Level 4: Further Maths applications including limits, coefficients, and series solutions to simple differential equations.

Core Formulas

  • Maclaurin: f(x) = f(0) + f'(0)x + f''(0)x² divided by 2! + f'''(0)x³ divided by 3! + ...
  • Taylor about a: f(x) = f(a) + f'(a)(x-a) + f''(a)(x-a)² divided by 2! + ...
  • When substituting into a known expansion, keep terms only up to the order requested.
