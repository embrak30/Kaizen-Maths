Teacher Instructions

Purpose

This tool generates practice on correlation, product moment correlation coefficient, least-squares regression lines, prediction, interpolation, and extrapolation.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: Read bivariate data tables, calculate and interpret correlation, and describe strength and direction.
  • Level 2: Calculate a least-squares regression line of y on x from raw data or summary statistics.
  • Level 3: Use regression lines for prediction, decide whether a prediction is interpolation or extrapolation, and interpret residuals.

Teaching Notes

  • The regression line used here is the least-squares line of y on x: ŷ = a + bx.
  • Use b = Sxy / Sxx and a = ȳ - bx̄.
  • Predictions inside the observed x-range are interpolation; predictions outside the observed x-range are extrapolation and should be treated with caution.
  • Correlation measures association, not causation.
