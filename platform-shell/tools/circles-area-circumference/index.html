Circle Area & Circumference Guidance

Overview

This tool focuses only on circumference and area of full circles. Sector area and arc length should stay as a separate later tool.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: Circumference from radius or diameter, plus reverse circumference.
  • Level 2: Area from radius or diameter, plus reverse area.
  • Level 3: Connect circumference and area in both directions.

Key Formulae

  • Circumference: C = 2πr or C = πd.
  • Area: A = πr^2.
  • Diameter and radius: d = 2r.

Common Misconceptions

  • Using diameter in A = πr^2 without halving it first.
  • Mixing circumference units and area units.
  • Forgetting that reverse area requires a square root.
  • Rounding too early before the final answer.
