Teacher Instructions

Overview

This tool provides scaffolded practice on common conversions across metric, imperial, time, compound measures, temperature, speed, and radians.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: One-step metric, imperial, time, area, volume, and basic degree-radian conversions.
  • Level 2: Formula and multi-step conversions including temperature, speed, density, mixed radians, and chain puzzles.

Common Misconceptions

  • Using a linear factor for area or volume conversions.
  • Forgetting to convert both numerator and denominator in compound units.
  • Dropping units or mixing unit systems mid-calculation.
  • Applying temperature scale factors instead of formulas.
