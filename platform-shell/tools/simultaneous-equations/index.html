Teacher Instructions

Getting Started

This tool helps students practise solving simultaneous linear equations in two variables using substitution and elimination. Problems are presented in LaTeX so working is clear when projected.

Level Guide

  • Level 1 - Substitution: Systems that are efficient to solve by substitution, including mixed practice.
  • Level 2 - Elimination: Direct, scaled, and harder elimination sets.

Teaching Strategies

  • Ask students to choose the most efficient method before solving.
  • Encourage full written working: rearrange, substitute, expand, collect, solve, and check.
  • Use mixed types for starters, fluency, and spaced retrieval.

Common Mistakes

  • Sign errors when adding or subtracting equations.
  • Incorrect distribution of negative signs when rearranging.
  • Stopping after finding one variable.
  • Dropping or mis-simplifying fractional coefficients and answers.

Standards and Alignment

  • UK KS3 / KS4: Simultaneous linear equations in two variables.
  • CCSS 8.EE.C.8: Analyse and solve pairs of simultaneous linear equations.
  • CCSS A.REI.C.6: Solve systems of linear equations exactly.
