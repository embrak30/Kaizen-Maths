Teacher Instructions

Overview

This tool focuses on advanced differentiation with two strands: implicit differentiation and parametric differentiation.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: dy/dx from F(x,y)=0, slopes at points, second derivatives, tangent lines, and related rates.
  • Level 2: dy/dx and d^2y/dx^2 from x(t), y(t), tangent and normal equations, and slope analysis.

Common Misconceptions

  • Treating y as a constant in implicit work.
  • Substituting a point before isolating the derivative.
  • Mixing parameter derivatives with Cartesian derivatives.
