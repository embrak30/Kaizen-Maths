TEACHER INSTRUCTIONS
Teacher Instructions

Purpose

This tool generates A-level style practice on reciprocal trigonometric functions. It covers sketching, equations, identities, proof, and hence-solve tasks.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: Graphs, reciprocal definitions, and simple equations involving sec, cosec, and cot.
  • Level 2: Equations that require identities, rearrangement, quadratics, and transformed angles.
  • Level 3: Identity proofs and linked proof-then-solve questions.

Teaching Notes

  • Students should connect sec, cosec, and cot to cosine, sine, and tangent before solving.
  • For equations, the worked steps show the identity or substitution before solving.
  • For proofs, the steps rewrite one side until it matches the other side.
  • Graph questions list key points and asymptotes rather than relying on a coloured diagram.
