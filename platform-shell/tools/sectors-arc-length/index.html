Sectors & Arc Length Guidance

Overview

This tool focuses on parts of circles: arc length and sector area. It is separate from the full circle area and circumference tool so teachers can target this topic directly.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: Arc length from radius and angle, plus reverse arc length problems.
  • Level 2: Sector area from radius and angle, plus reverse sector area problems.
  • Level 3: Mixed links between arc length and sector area.

Key Formulae

  • Arc length: L = θ/360 × 2πr.
  • Sector area: A = θ/360 × πr^2.
  • Link: when radius and arc length are known, A = 1/2 rL.

Common Misconceptions

  • Using the full circumference or full area without multiplying by θ/360.
  • Confusing arc length units with sector area units.
  • Using diameter instead of radius in sector formulae.
  • Rounding too early in reverse problems.
