Teacher Instructions

Getting Started

This tool helps students practise converting between fractions, decimals, and percentages. Each row gives one representation and asks students to calculate the other two.

How It Works

  • Given values: one value is shown in each row.
  • Calculate: students find the missing fraction, decimal, or percentage values.
  • Interactive reveal: click a missing cell to show one answer, or reveal all answers at once.

Level Guide

  • Basic: common equivalents such as halves, quarters, fifths, and tenths.
  • Intermediate: thirds, sixths, eighths, ninths, and elevenths.
  • Advanced: more challenging recurring decimals and less familiar fractions.

Key Conversion Methods

  • Fraction to decimal: divide numerator by denominator.
  • Decimal to percentage: multiply by 100.
  • Percentage to decimal: divide by 100.
  • Decimal to fraction: write over a power of 10, then simplify.
  • Percentage to fraction: write over 100, then simplify.
