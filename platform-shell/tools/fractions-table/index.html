Teacher Instructions

Purpose

Use this tool for quick starter tables, retrieval practice, mini-whiteboard checks, or short whole-class questioning.

How It Works

  • Choose a table type from the dropdown.
  • Some cells are already shown and some are hidden behind question marks.
  • Click a hidden cell to reveal one answer at a time.
  • Use Show All or Hide Answers when you want to check the full table quickly.

Table Types

  • Fractions, decimals, percentages
  • Area and perimeter of rectangles
  • Area of 2D shapes
  • Volume and surface area
  • Algebraic expansion and factorising
  • Sequences, ratio, probability, standard form, and Pythagoras/trigonometry
