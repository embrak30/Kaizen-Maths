Getting Started

This tool helps students practise quadratic equations with a structured progression. Each level focuses on a specific form and solution method, with mixed sets for fluency practice.

Level Guide

  • Level 1 - Basic Squares: Equations of the form x^2 = k or equivalent rearrangements.
  • Level 2 - Factorable: Quadratics solved by factoring and the zero product property.
  • Level 3 - Non-Factorable: General quadratics requiring the quadratic formula.
  • Level 4 - Non-Standard / Disguised: Rearrangement or substitution before solving.

Assessment Focus

  • Choosing square-root, factoring, or quadratic formula appropriately.
  • Recognising standard form and disguised quadratic structure.
  • Interpreting the discriminant and rounding formula answers to 2 decimal places.
