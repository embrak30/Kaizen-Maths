Teacher Instructions

Getting Started

This tool builds fluency with addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division through progressive practice and real-world contexts.

Level Structure

  • Level 1: Single-digit facts and related division facts.
  • Level 2: Two-digit operations and standard written methods.
  • Level 3: Larger multiplication and division with estimation and checking.
  • Level 4: Shopping, measurement, and everyday word problems.

Teaching Focus

  • Ask pupils to name the operation before calculating.
  • Use estimation to check whether answers are reasonable.
  • Use Steps to model written methods and operation choice.
  • Build speed only after accuracy is secure.
