📐 Teacher Instructions

Getting Started

This tool helps students practise sequences and nth term formulas at different difficulty levels. Perfect for whole-class projection and individual practice.

US Math Standards Covered

8.F.A.1 - Understand that a function assigns exactly one output to each input.

F-IF.A.3 - Recognize sequences as functions whose domain is a subset of integers.

F-BF.A.2 - Write arithmetic and geometric sequences recursively and explicitly.

Level Guide

  • Level 1 - Basic Sequences: Finding next terms in arithmetic and geometric sequences.
  • Level 2 - nth Term Formulas: Finding and using nth term formulas for linear and quadratic sequences.

Sequence Types Covered

  • Arithmetic: Constant difference.
  • Geometric: Constant ratio.
  • Linear nth term: an + b formulas.
  • Quadratic nth term: an² + bn + c formulas.

Teaching Tips

  • Start with Level 1 and specific sequence types.
  • Use Steps to model systematic formula derivation.
  • Compare arithmetic, geometric, and quadratic patterns.
  • Connect formulas to position numbers, not just term values.
