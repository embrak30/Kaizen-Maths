Teacher Instructions

Getting Started

This tool helps students practise HCF and LCM in a structured progression from simple integers to algebra and word problems.

Level Guide

  • Level 1 - Basic Numbers: Simple HCF and LCM with small integers.
  • Level 2 - Algebra: HCF and LCM with algebraic expressions.
  • Level 3 - Mixed Problems: A mixture of numerical and algebraic prompts.
  • Level 4 - Word Problems: Real-world applications that require choosing HCF or LCM.

Teaching Strategies

  • Ask students to decide first whether a situation calls for a factor or a multiple.
  • Use prime factorisation to compare powers clearly.
  • For algebra, reinforce that HCF uses the lowest shared power and LCM uses the highest required power.

Common Mistakes

  • Confusing HCF with LCM.
  • Missing repeated prime factors.
  • Forgetting variables or using the wrong variable powers in algebraic expressions.
  • Not checking whether a word problem needs grouping or synchronising.
