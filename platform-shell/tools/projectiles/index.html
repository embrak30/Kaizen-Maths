Teacher Instructions

Purpose

This tool generates projectile motion practice for resolving velocity, horizontal and vertical modelling, range, maximum height, and simple projectile applications.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: Resolve launch velocity and calculate basic time, height, and range values.
  • Level 2: Use horizontal and vertical equations together to find position, speed, and height at a distance.
  • Level 3: Model applied projectile questions such as clearing a wall, finding launch speed, or finding possible launch angles.

Teaching Notes

  • Horizontal velocity is constant when air resistance is ignored.
  • Vertical acceleration is -9.8 m/s² when upward is positive.
  • Split every problem into horizontal and vertical components before substituting.
