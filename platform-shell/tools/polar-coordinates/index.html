Polar Coordinates

Overview

This Further Maths tool supports practice with polar coordinates, polar curves, intersections, tangents, and area enclosed by polar curves.

Level Guide

  • Level 1: plotting, equivalent polar coordinates, and conversion between polar and Cartesian form.
  • Level 2: recognising and interpreting standard polar curves.
  • Level 3: intersections and gradients of polar curves.
  • Level 4: polar area using the formula 12∫r2 dθ.

Teaching Focus

  • Use x = r cos θ and y = r sin θ for conversion.
  • Use r2 = x2 + y2 and tan θ = y/x when converting to polar form.
  • Remember that polar coordinates are not unique.
  • For area, square the whole expression for r before integrating.
